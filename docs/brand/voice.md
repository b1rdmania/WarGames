# Voice

## Tone

**Terse.** Every word earns its place.

**Cold-blooded.** War is the input. Trade is the output.

**Confident.** We don't hedge or qualify. We state.

## Rules

1. Short sentences. Break long ones.
2. Active voice. "You click" not "the button is clicked."
3. No qualifiers. Drop "just," "simply," "basically."
4. No marketing speak. No "revolutionary" or "game-changing."
5. No emojis in product copy. Ever.

## Examples

### Good

- "Taiwan goes hot. TSMC shuts. Nvidia dies."
- "Trade the tension."
- "One thesis. One click. One position."
- "Real money. Real risk."

### Bad

- "Revolutionary trading experience for savvy investors!"
- "Easy-peasy macro plays for everyone ðŸš€"
- "Join thousands of traders maximizing their potential"
- "Simply click the button to start your journey"

## Headlines

State the thesis. No fluff.

| Bad | Good |
|-----|------|
| "Introducing our amazing new feature" | "Basket trades are live" |
| "The ultimate guide to trading" | "How to trade" |
| "Revolutionizing the way you invest" | "Trade narratives" |

## Error messages

Be direct. Say what happened. Say what to do.

| Bad | Good |
|-----|------|
| "Oops! Something went wrong" | "Trade failed: insufficient balance" |
| "Please try again later" | "Market closed. Opens Mon 9:30am ET" |

## Buttons

Verbs. Short. Clear.

| Bad | Good |
|-----|------|
| "Click here to connect" | "Connect" |
| "Submit your trade" | "Trade" |
| "Learn more about this" | "Details" |

## Numbers

Use numerals. No spelling out.

| Bad | Good |
|-----|------|
| "Three times leverage" | "3x leverage" |
| "Two hundred dollars" | "$200" |

## Punctuation

Periods after statements. No exclamation marks in product copy.

Headlines: no period.
Body: periods.
Lists: no periods if single line, periods if multiple sentences.
